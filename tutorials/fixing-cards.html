Fixing cards that do not render

Most render problems come from the AI's formatting, not from ChemRender. Here are the common causes and quick fixes.

Common issues

  • Invalid SMILES. The molecule string is malformed and cannot be drawn.
  • Missing ::mol marker. Each card must start with the ::mol line.
  • Broken indentation. Sections like lone pairs, charges, and arrows rely on consistent indentation.
  • Missing title or caption. A card without a title or caption can look empty or incomplete.
  • Impossible chemistry. Sometimes the AI invents a structure that cannot exist.

The quickest fix: copy the error prompt to your AI

When a card does not render, use the copy error prompt button in the app. It builds a prompt that includes the error and what went wrong. Send that to your AI tutor first — not to me — and ask it to fix the answer in ChemRender molecule-card format. The AI usually corrects the SMILES, marker, or formatting in one step.

If it still does not render

  • Paste the error prompt back to the AI again and ask it to use valid SMILES and double-check the structure.
  • Ask it to keep one molecule per card and re-include the ::mol marker.
  • Ask it to add a short title and caption to each card.
  • Still stuck after trying the AI? Then email me a screenshot of the broken card so I can fix the renderer.
