How it works

From an AI answer to study cards in five steps.

1

Download ChemRender

Install the free app for macOS or Windows.

2

Copy the built-in prompt

Open the app and copy the built-in ChemRender prompt — no formatting to memorize.

3

Ask your AI tutor

Ask ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or another AI an organic chemistry question.

4

Paste the response

Paste the AI's answer back into ChemRender.

5

Study the cards

Review the rendered molecule cards with structures, charges, and captions.

Built for studying

Designed to disappear while you study.

ChemRender should not become another thing to manage. Import an answer, study the cards, and keep going. Keyboard shortcuts make import, replace, navigation, and export quick. Flexible keybinds let students use simple controls, Vim-style controls, or their own setup.

  • One-button import and paste workflow
  • Fast replace flow for fixing one card
  • Keyboard navigation between rendered cards
  • Flexible keybind presets and custom controls
